# Paejae Pick 2.0 v0.7 Department Nasumi Tour

## Goal

v0.7 adds the Department Nasumi Tour.

This feature helps students explore department buildings and understand the campus structure through collectible department-themed Nasumi cards.

## Problem

Many students only know their own department building, dormitory, cafeteria, and frequently used classrooms.

Especially:

- Freshmen do not know the campus layout well.
- Commuting students do not stay on campus long.
- Students often do not know where department offices are.
- Students rarely visit buildings outside their own department.
- Some students have difficulty finding professor offices or administrative offices.

## Solution

Each department or major campus building can have a QR/code mission.

When students visit that building and enter the code, they collect a department Nasumi card.

This creates a reason to walk around campus.

## Example Flow

1. Student opens Paejae Pick.
2. Student checks today's Department Nasumi Tour.
3. Student visits the building.
4. Student enters the department code.
5. Student collects the department Nasumi card.
6. Student learns building information such as department office floor and major facilities.

## v0.7 Scope

- Department Nasumi Tour screen
- Department Nasumi cards
- Department building information
- Department mission codes
- Local collection integration
- Home entry card

## Privacy

This feature does not track real-time location.

v0.7 uses local code input only.

No student movement path is stored on a server.
