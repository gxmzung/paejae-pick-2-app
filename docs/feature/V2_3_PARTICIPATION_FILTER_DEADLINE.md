# 배재Pick 2.0 v2.3 Participation Filter / Deadline Sort

## Goal

v2.3 adds the concept of filtering and deadline-based sorting to the Campus Participation Hub.

The goal is to help students quickly find what they can join now.

## Filter Types

- All
- Club Recruitment
- Department / Office Event
- Project Team
- Volunteer / Supporters
- Contest Team
- Deadline Soon

## Sorting Rules

- Deadline-soon notices should appear higher.
- Visible notices are shown in the main list.
- Closed notices are separated.
- Under-review notices are hidden from general students.
- Hidden notices are removed from the list.

## Why

If the participation hub becomes just another long list, it fails.

The product should reduce search cost.

Students should not browse randomly.

They should quickly answer:

What can I join this week?

## Product Principle

The Campus Participation Hub is not an anonymous board.

It is a structured recruitment and participation infrastructure.
