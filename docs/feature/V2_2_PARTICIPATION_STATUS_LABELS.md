# 배재Pick 2.0 v2.2 Participation Status Labels

## Goal

v2.2 defines status labels for Campus Participation Hub notices.

The goal is to separate host-submitted notices from verified or visible notices.

## Status Labels

### 1. Host Submitted

The host has submitted a notice request.

### 2. Info Check Needed

Important information is missing or unclear.

### 3. Under Review

The app operator or responsible reviewer is checking the notice.

### 4. Visible

The notice is visible to students.

### 5. Closed

Recruitment has ended.

### 6. Hidden

The notice is temporarily hidden due to an issue.

## Important Principle

Checked does not mean officially approved.

Official approval belongs to the university, department, club, or host organization.

## Responsibility Split

The host handles:

- Venue
- Budget
- Safety
- On-site operation
- Conflict handling
- Official approval

The app handles:

- Notice state
- Visibility
- Record keeping
- Interest saving
- Contact/application flow
