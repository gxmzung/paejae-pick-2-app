# 배재Pick 2.0 v2.0 Campus Participation Hub

## Goal

v2.0 reframes the club notice hall as a campus participation infrastructure.

The app is not trying to directly operate events.

The goal is to prevent student participation information from disappearing when student council or club council structures are vacant.

## Core Position

배재Pick은 행사를 주최하지 않는다.

배재Pick은 학생 참여 정보를 구조화해 보여주는 공고 허브다.

## Scope

- Rename the direction from club-only notice hall to campus participation hub
- Support club recruitment as the first use case
- Prepare expansion toward department events, project teams, volunteer groups, supporters, and contest teams
- Add in-app explanation that the app is infrastructure, not an event operator

## What the App Handles

- Notice structure
- Recruitment visibility
- Interest saving
- Application/contact flow
- Status display
- Record keeping

## What the App Does Not Handle

- Venue reservation
- Budget execution
- Safety responsibility
- Official school approval
- On-site staffing
- Conflict judgment
- Official representation

## Product Principle

The app should never become an anonymous board.

It should remain a structured participation notice hub.

## Recommended Public Framing

배재Pick은 총학생회나 총동아리회를 대체하지 않습니다.

학생자치 공백 상황에서도 동아리 모집, 행사 참여, 프로젝트 팀 모집 정보가 흩어지지 않도록 돕는 참여 인프라입니다.
