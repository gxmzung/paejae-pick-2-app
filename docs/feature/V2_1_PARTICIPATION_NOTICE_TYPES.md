# 배재Pick 2.0 v2.1 Participation Notice Types

## Goal

v2.1 defines the notice type model for the Campus Participation Hub.

The app should not be limited to club recruitment.

It should support multiple structured campus participation notices.

## Notice Types

### 1. Club Recruitment

- Club member recruitment
- Department club recruitment
- Project club recruitment

### 2. Department / Office Event

- Department events
- Special lectures
- Info sessions
- Campus booths

### 3. Project Team

- App development team
- Research project team
- Startup team
- Major project team

### 4. Volunteer / Supporters

- Event staff
- Volunteer group
- Promotion team
- Supporters recruitment

### 5. Contest Team

- Competition team
- Hackathon team
- Idea contest team
- Paper / presentation team

## Core Principle

Paejae Pick does not operate events.

Paejae Pick structures and exposes participation notices.

## Responsibility Split

The host handles:

- Venue
- Budget
- Safety
- On-site operation
- Conflict handling
- Official approval

The app handles:

- Notice structure
- Visibility
- Interest saving
- Contact/application flow
- Status display
- Record keeping
