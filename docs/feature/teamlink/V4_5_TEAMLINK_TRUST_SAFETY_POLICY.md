# 배재Pick 2.0 v4.5 TeamLink Trust / Safety Policy

## Goal

v4.5 adds the trust and safety layer for TeamLink.

TeamLink is not just a team recruitment feature.

Because it connects students, it needs rules for:

- trust
- reporting
- privacy
- official notice separation
- conflict limitation

## Scope

This version adds:

- TeamLink safety policy screen
- report reason examples
- trust label rules
- privacy and responsibility warnings
- safety policy entry button from TeamLink

## Trust Rules

TeamLink must separate:

1. official university notices
2. external official contest notices
3. student-created recruitment posts
4. mock/sample data

## Recommended Labels

- 학교 공식
- 외부 공식
- 학생 모집글
- 확인 필요
- mock
- 모집 중
- 마감 임박
- 모집 완료

## Report Reasons

Examples:

- fake recruitment
- pretending to be an official notice
- excessive personal information request
- inappropriate language
- money request
- unsafe external link
- team conflict or harassment

## Privacy Rules

TeamLink should avoid exposing:

- phone number
- student ID
- private KakaoTalk ID
- unnecessary personal data

Safer alternatives:

- alias
- app internal message
- controlled contact field
- optional portfolio link

## Important Limitation

TeamLink can connect students.

TeamLink cannot:

- guarantee teammate quality
- guarantee contest awards
- resolve all team conflicts
- verify every student's ability
- replace official contest guidance

## Product Positioning

TeamLink lowers the first barrier to forming a team.

It should not pretend to manage the entire team lifecycle.
