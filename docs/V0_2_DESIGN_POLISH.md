# Paejae Pick 2.0 v0.2 Design Polish

## Goal

v0.2 improves the first impression of the Flutter MVP.

The app should feel closer to a real campus app, not just a Flutter shell.

## Scope

- Splash screen
- Login screen mock
- Start flow
- Nasumi visual identity
- Home design polish
- Campus exploration tone

## App Identity

Paejae Pick 2.0 is a smart campus app for Paichai University students.

It connects:

- 3D campus map
- Nasumi campus collection
- QR/code missions
- club recruitment notices
- CityBrain cafeteria preview
- campus alert and facility reports
- future admin console

## Design Direction

- Student-friendly
- Bright campus tone
- Rounded card UI
- Blue main color
- Yellow Nasumi reward accent
- Green mission/exploration accent
- Not too game-like
- Still acceptable as a school-facing service
